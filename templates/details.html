Precautions & Operational Guidance

Interpreting Model Output

The model outputs a probability map and a summary risk score. Use these guidelines:

  • HIGH — Immediate action recommended. Expect significant flooding; follow local evacuation orders.
  • MEDIUM — Monitor conditions; prepare to move to safety if advised.
  • LOW — No immediate flooding indicated, but keep monitoring official sources.

Practical Precautions

  • Avoid driving through flooded roads — six inches of water can stall a car.
  • Move important documents and electronics to higher ground.
  • Follow local emergency services and official weather alerts.
  • Do not attempt to swim or wade through fast-moving water.

Limitations

  • Model accuracy depends on SAR preprocessing and sensor calibration.
  • Georeferencing errors in input images will affect overlay placement.
  • This tool provides probabilistic outputs and should be used with caution.

Further Work

Options to improve operational readiness include automated alerting, integration with local hydrological gauges, and multi-temporal change detection.
