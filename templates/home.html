Flood Prediction using SAR Imagery

About

This project performs flood detection using Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) imagery. SAR is ideal for flood mapping because it can "see" through clouds and during night-time, providing reliable water extent estimates.

How it works

  1. Upload a georeferenced SAR GeoTIFF or click a location on the map.
  2. The model analyzes radar backscatter to segment potential flooded areas.
  3. The backend converts predicted mask regions into GeoJSON and computes risk statistics.

Disclaimer

This system is provided for research and demonstration only. Predictions are probabilistic and should not be used as the sole basis for life-or-death decisions. Validate with authoritative local sources and official warnings.
